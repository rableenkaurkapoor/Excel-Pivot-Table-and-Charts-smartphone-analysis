# Smartphone Sales Trend Analysis in Excel

## Project Overview

In this project, I used Excel PivotTables and PivotCharts to analyze smartphone sales data by creating annual, quarterly, and monthly views to identify trends and patterns over time.

## Objective

The main objective of this project was to study sales performance at different time levels and understand how trends change when viewed yearly, quarterly, and monthly.

## Tools Used

Excel  
PivotTables  
PivotCharts  
Line Charts  

## Analysis Performed

### Annual Sales Trend
Created a PivotTable and PivotChart to summarize smartphone sales by year.

**Insight:**  
Sales increased steadily from 2018 to 2021, showing consistent growth over time.

### Quarterly Sales Trend
Created a PivotTable and PivotChart to compare smartphone sales across quarters.

**Insight:**  
Sales were highest in Quarter 4 and lowest in Quarter 2, showing stronger performance toward the end of the year.

### Monthly Sales Trend
Created a PivotTable and PivotChart to analyze month by month sales patterns.

**Insight:**  
Sales fluctuated across the year, with the highest value in October and lower values in months such as January and May.

## Key Learning

This project helped strengthen my understanding of how Excel can be used to group time based data, create trend focused visualizations, and generate business insights from raw sales information.
